The walk

The walk from Monterosso to Riomaggiore will take you approximately two hours, give o take hor dependning on the weather conditions and your physical shape.

WHAT?

Clingue Terre comprises five villages : Monterresso,Vernazza, Cornigila,Manarola and Riomaggiore

WHERE?

On the northwest of the Rivera,north of the city La Sao

PRICE?

The walk is free
